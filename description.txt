[h1]Welcome to Terraria, Shrouded in Mist...[/h1]
[b]"Every journey has an end, and our destination is a sea of stars."[/b]

This mod brings the Beyonder system from "Lord of the Mysteries" to Terraria!
Abandon vanilla class restrictions, consume potions, complete rituals to advance sequences, and master mythical powers. The current version includes five Beyonder pathways: Marauder, Giant (Warrior), Red Priest, Moon, and Fool.

[hr]

[h1]üîÆ Core Features[/h1]

[h2]1. Unique Spirituality System[/h2]
[list]
[*]Introduces an independent [Spirituality] system.
[*]Unlike vanilla Mana, Spirituality is the foundation for casting Beyonder abilities.
[*]Spirituality cap increases exponentially with sequence advancement, from the faint spirituality of Sequence 9 to the ocean-like spirituality of Sequence 1.
[/list]

[h2]2. Five Beyonder Pathways[/h2]
Currently, five pathways are implemented, offering a complete progression experience from Sequence 9 to Sequence 1 (Note: Giant pathway is missing Sequence 2):

[b]New: Marauder Pathway[/b]
[i]"All rules have loopholes, and all fate can be stolen."[/i]
[list]
[*]Parasitizing & Body Seizing: You can parasitize enemies or NPCs, or instantly transform your body into countless Worms of Spirit to reform, thereby dodging fatal strikes.
[*]Authority of Theft: Not just stealing items‚Äîyou can even steal enemies' health, mana, buffs, and even their "attack range" and "time."
[*]Deceit Domain: Distort the rules of reality, deflecting enemy attacks or plunging surrounding creatures into chaos and self-doubt.
[*]Worm of Time: As a Sequence 1 King of Angels, you can summon the phantom of an ancient clock, slowing down time around you and even stripping enemies of their lifespan to age them.
[*]Role: Thief/Survival/Control, weakening enemies, toying with time and fate.
[/list]

[b]Fool Pathway (Seer)[/b]
[i]"Fooling time, deceiving history."[/i]
[list]
[*]Spirit Body Threads: Forcibly control enemy actions and completely convert them into loyal Marionettes (minions).
[*]Historical Projection: Summon phantoms of defeated bosses from the fog of history to aid in battle.
[*]Miracle Wish: Grant wishes to yourself to achieve health reset, apocalyptic calamities, instant teleportation, or day/night cycle changes.
[*]Role: Summoner, powerful crowd control, unpredictability, and conceptual immortality.
[/list]

[b]Giant Pathway (Warrior)[/b]
[i]"Protection is the glory of a Giant."[/i]
[list]
[*]Dawn Armor: Summon armor of light, providing extremely high defense and damage reduction.
[*]Twilight Resurrection: Sequence 2 ability. Upon death, reverse time to reset your state (long cooldown).
[*]Mercury Form: Transform into mercury, significantly boosting mobility and evasion.
[*]Role: Melee, ultra-high tankiness, extremely high fault tolerance.
[/list]

[b]Red Priest Pathway (Hunter)[/b]
[i]"The best hunter often appears in the form of prey."[/i]
[list]
[*]Army of One: Significantly increases minion slots based on sequence level (Sequence 1 can add 40 slots!).
[*]Weakness Break: Extremely high critical damage bonuses and execution capabilities.
[*]Weather Warlock: Summon thunder and fog to alter the battlefield environment.
[*]Conqueror: Advance through a special ritual to gain absolute suppression over enemies.
[*]Role: Summoner/Ranger hybrid, high burst damage, group combat.
[/list]

[b]Moon Pathway[/b]
[i]"The Moon represents life, but also terror."[/i]
[list]
[*]Summoning Gate: Open the gate to the Spirit World to summon random powerful creatures (with a very small chance to summon projections of Old Gods...).
[*]Creation Domain: Unfold a domain that heals allies and continuously damages enemies.
[*]Vampire Avatar: Transform into a swarm of bats or moonlight, gaining unparalleled survival capabilities.
[*]Beauty Goddess: Charm all living beings, causing enemies to fight each other or lose the will to fight.
[*]Role: Mage/Summoner, powerful healing, crowd control master.
[/list]

[hr]

[h1]Gameplay Guide[/h1]

1. Recipes & Crafting: [Blasphemy Slates] will randomly spawn in treasure chests, recording potion recipes. Find special Beyonder materials (such as characteristics, blood, etc.) to craft potions.
2. Advancement Mechanism: Some high sequences (e.g., Sequence 2 -> Sequence 1) require not only potions but also the completion of acting rituals (e.g., Hunters need to clear the map of all rivals).
3. Keybinds:
   Please bind Mod hotkeys in settings to use active skills!
   [list]
   [*][b]J Key[/b] (Default): Triggers core skills (e.g., Paper Figurine Substitute, Form Switching).
   [*][b]Skill Keys[/b]: Used to unleash ultimate abilities (e.g., Summoning Gate, Ice Age).
   [*][b]Check "Controls" settings for more keybinds.[/b]
   [/list]

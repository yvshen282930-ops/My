[h1]Welcome to a Terraria Shrouded in Mist...[/h1] [b]"Every journey has its endpoint, and our endpoint is the sea of stars."[/b]

This Mod brings the Beyonder system from [i]Lord of the Mysteries[/i] into Terraria! Abandon the vanilla class restrictions, consume potions, complete rituals to advance through Sequences, and master god-like powers. The current version includes five Beyonder pathways: Error, Giant (Warrior), Red Priest, Moon, and Fool.

[hr]

[h1]üîÆ Core Features[/h1]

[h2]1. Unique Spirituality System[/h2] [list] []Introduces an independent [b][Spirituality][/b] system. []Unlike vanilla Mana, Spirituality is the foundation for casting Beyonder abilities. [*]Max Spirituality grows exponentially with Sequence advancement, ranging from the faint spirituality of Sequence 9 to the ocean-like vastness of Sequence 1. [/list]

[h2]2. Five Beyonder Pathways[/h2] Currently implements five pathways with a complete progression experience from Sequence 9 to Sequence 1 (Note: The Giant pathway currently lacks Sequence 2 content):

[b]Error Pathway (Marauder)[/b] [i]"All rules have loopholes, and all fate can be stolen."[/i] [list] []Parasitizing & Body Seizing: You can parasitize enemies or NPCs, or instantly disperse your body into countless Spirit Worms to reform and evade fatal strikes. []Authority of Theft: Steal not just items, but your enemy's health, mana, buffs, and even their "Attack Range" and "Time". []Deceit Domain: Distort the rules of reality, deflecting enemy attacks or plunging surrounding creatures into chaos and self-doubt. []Worm of Time: As a Sequence 1 King of Angels, summon an ancient clock phantom to slow down the flow of time and strip away enemies' lifespan, causing them to age. [*]Role: Thief, Survival, Crowd Control, Debuff, Time & Fate manipulation. [/list]

[b]Fool Pathway (Seer)[/b] [i]"Fooling time, deceiving fate."[/i] [list] []Spirit Body Threads: Forcibly control enemy actions and convert them into loyal Marionettes (Summons). []Historical Projections: Summon phantoms of previously defeated Bosses from the void of history to aid you. []Miracles & Wishes: Grant wishes to yourself to reset health, unleash cataclysms, teleport instantly, or change day and night. []Role: Summoner, Strong Crowd Control, Versatility, Conceptual Immortality. [/list]

[b]Giant Pathway (Warrior)[/b] [i]"Protection is the glory of a Giant."[/i] [list] []Dawn Armor: Summon armor of light, providing immense defense and damage reduction. []Twilight Resurrection: Sequence 2 ability. Upon death, reverse time to reset your state (Long Cooldown). []Mercury Form: Transform into liquid mercury, drastically boosting mobility and evasion. []Role: Melee, Super Tank, High Fault Tolerance. [/list]

[b]Red Priest Pathway (Hunter)[/b] [i]"The best hunters often appear as prey."[/i] [list] []Army of One: Drastically increases minion slots based on Sequence level (Sequence 1 grants +40 slots!). []Weakness Break: Extremely high critical damage bonuses and execution capabilities. []Weather Warlock: Summon thunder and fog to alter the battlefield environment. []Conqueror: Advance through a special ritual to gain absolute suppression over enemies. [*]Role: Summoner/Ranged Hybrid, High Burst, Group Warfare. [/list]

[b]Moon Pathway[/b] [i]"The Moon represents life, but also terror."[/i] [list] []Summoning Door: Open a gate to the Spirit World to summon random powerful creatures (with a slim chance to summon projections of Ancient Gods...). []Creation Domain: Unfold a domain that heals teammates and continuously damages enemies. []Vampire Incarnation: Transform into a swarm of bats or moonlight for unparalleled survival capabilities. []Beauty Goddess: Charm all living things, causing enemies to fight each other or lose the will to fight. [*]Role: Mage/Summoner, Strong Healing, Crowd Control Master. [/list]

[b]New Addition: Visionary Dog (Dog)[/b] [i]"Maybe my pet's Sequence is higher than yours."[/i] [list] []Can consume potions from the five currently available pathways. []Features both "Follow" and "Sentry" modes. []Stats can be upgraded by using Life Crystals and equipping Accessories. []Summoned using a Dog Tag. [*]Name is customizable. [/list]

[h1]Gameplay Instructions (How to Play)[/h1]

[b]Recipes & Crafting:[/b] [Blasphemy Slates] will randomly appear in Chests, Fishing Crates, and Boss drops. They record potion recipes. Gather special Beyonder materials (like characteristics, blood, etc.) to craft potions.

[b]Advancement Mechanism:[/b] High Sequences (e.g., Seq 2 -> Seq 1) require not just potions, but also the completion of "Acting" rituals (e.g., The Hunter needs to be unrivaled on the map).

[b]Keybindings:[/b] Please bind Mod Hotkeys in the settings to use active skills! [list] [][b]J Key[/b] (Default): Core skill trigger (e.g., Paper Figurine, Form Switching). [][b]Skill Key[/b]: Used for Ultimate abilities (e.g., Summoning Door, Ice Age). [*]Check the "Controls" settings for more keybinds. [/list]
